About our Company

Avecita Green Cleaning was established in 2022. It started as a small business that eventually grew and is committed to offer good quality cleaning services. As a company we make an effort in taking care of our environment by utilizing eco-friendly products, reducing the negative effects that certain chemicals have in our community, pets and our health.

Why Green Cleaning?


Conventional cleaning products contain chemicals such as chlorine and ammonia that when mixed together can be bad for your health. Small children and pets are especially vulnerable, which is a reason for us to use green cleaning products that aren't toxic or as dangerous. Additionally by using these products, we help to reduce the harm we as humans do to our environment.
